Data Scientist

Data scientist is one of the most high-level positions in IT, combining statistics, mathematics and computer science. Companies hire these professionals to analyse and extract information from large data sets to support operations and business strategy.

IT Project Manager

A position that combines technological operations with general management skills. It is responsible for looking after a project from its birth to completion. Their main task is to create and coordinate a team with the right skills and experience to deliver the project on time and to specification.

Full-Stack Developer

These elite professionals move seamlessly between front-end and back-end processes. Full-stack developers also need to be masters of communication in order to engage with a diverse range of clients and stakeholders, as well as being accountable to management.

Cyber Security Analyst

Analysts are the 'first line of defence' - their job is to monitor an organisation's network to detect security breaches and vulnerabilities. Based on this analysis, cyber security architects and engineers can design and implement systems to prevent the identified threats.

AI Specialist

The work of an AI specialist involves the design, implementation and development of artificial intelligence systems. It deals with analysing data, creating machine learning algorithms and designing automation and robotics systems.

Business Analyst

Software Engineer
